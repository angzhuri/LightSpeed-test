Go deep to go far

Too often VC only scratches the surface. Endless jacks of all trades and masters of none. Continually consuming until there’s a basic understanding of everything and expertise in nothing. Lightspeed takes a different approach.

We value substance over shininess…Unwavering depth over breadth of scope…Creating together over funding from afar…And investing time and energy, not just dollars.

Depth is our center of gravity. It’s how we build and maintain a mastery that makes a difference for founders. Constantly growing our conviction, curiosity, and understanding to continually earn our seat at the table. Getting on the same level as our founders, investing early, and growing alongside them.

Complete immersion is the heart of our philosophy on investing and what separates us from speculators and opportunists. Dedicating ourselves to our founders’ businesses and growing with them means we can build something truly impactful.

Because possibility grows the deeper you go.
